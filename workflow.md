# Workflow

## Objective

Produce a technical feasibility report from source drafts, evidence, and reproducible models.

## Rules

- Preserve every technical detail before rewriting.
- Label each claim as direct evidence, same-class evidence, model assumption, pending, or rejected.
- Do not promote source-draft estimates into verified facts.
- Use first-principles process structure before prose polish.
- Compare alternatives at the same layer.
- Keep process language out of final drafts.

## Evidence Grades

- `F0`: basic physical fact or industry convention requiring final citation.
- `E1`: direct measured data with test conditions.
- `E2`: official/vendor/conference claim with partial technical detail.
- `E3`: same-class literature or benchmark.
- `E4`: model estimate or assumption.
- `PENDING`: important but not supported enough.

## Deliverable Checklist

- Source inventory completed.
- `technical_points/ledger.csv` updated.
- Evidence registry updated.
- Model assumptions and outputs stored in `integration/`.
- Draft rendered or compiled.
- Remaining unknowns listed explicitly.
